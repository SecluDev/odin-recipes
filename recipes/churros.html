Churros Recipe

These churros are crispy on the outside, yet wonderfully tender on the inside..

This restaurant-worthy churro recipe will quickly become a staple in your dessert rotation.

Ingredients:

Original recipe (1X) yields 4 servings

  • 1 cup water

  • 2 ½ tablespoons white sugar

  • ½ teaspoon salt

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 2 quarts oil for frying

  • ½ cup white sugar, or to taste

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.

    2.

  2. Combine water, 2 ½ tablespoons sugar, salt, and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a small saucepan

    and place over medium heat. Bring to a boil and remove from the heat.

    3.

  3. Stir in flour, stirring until mixture forms a ball.

    4.

  4. Heat oil for frying in a deep fryer or deep pot to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

    Transfer dough to a sturdy pastry bag fitted with a medium star tip.

    5.

  5. Carefully pipe a few 5- to 6-inch strips of dough into the hot oil; work in batches so you don't crowd the fryer.

    6.

  6. Cook until golden; use a spider or slotted spoon to transfer churros to paper towels to drain.

    7.

  7. Combine 1/2 cup sugar and cinnamon. Roll drained churros in cinnamon and sugar mixture.

    8.
